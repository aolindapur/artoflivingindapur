About the Center

The Art of Living Meditation Center, Indapur is a peaceful and positive space dedicated to meditation, yoga, breathing techniques, and holistic personality development. The center helps people experience calmness, clarity, confidence, and joy in daily life.

Center Ambience

The center offers a clean, calm, and spiritually uplifting ambience. It supports deep meditation, inner silence, and connection with higher consciousness.

Regular Programs

  • Happiness Program (Sudarshan Kriya)
  • Utkarsha Yoga and Medha Yoga
  • Intuition Process Level 1 and 2
  • Yoga & Pranayama Sessions
  • Weekly Group Meditation & Satsang
